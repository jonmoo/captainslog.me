--- layout: post title: Force the use of 'www' in your URL. tags: - apache - quickfire status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '2' --- This is a pretty simple use of mod_rewrite to force the use www in your urls.
RewriteEngine On RewriteCond %{HTTP_HOST} !^www\.example\.com$ [NC] RewriteRule .? http://www.example.com%{REQUEST_URI} [R=301,L]\
The first line is only needed if mod_rewrite isn't enabled on your server (though the module does need to be enabled). Replace example.com with your actual domain name of course.
