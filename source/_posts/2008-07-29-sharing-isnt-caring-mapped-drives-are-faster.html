Mapped drives are faster. tags: - file sharing - sp3 - tips - windows status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '4' --- According to this a mapped Windows drive is a lot faster for transferring files than simply browsing to the share. Find your share under Network Places, right-click and select "Map Network Drive". Some other tips: put a $ at the end of your share name to hide it. Upgrade to SP3 to have multiple shares with different permissions on a folder.
